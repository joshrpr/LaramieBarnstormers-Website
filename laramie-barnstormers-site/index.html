Safety and facility use

Club rules

Flying site safety rules

  1. Deliberate flying behind the safety line is prohibited.
  2. It is strongly recommended that members do not fly alone.
  3. Children are NOT permitted beyond the spectator area unless under the direct supervision of an adult or under flight instruction.
  4. Sailplane high start lines are to run parallel to active runway.
  5. Direction of flight is determined by the wind direction, etc.
  6. Flight operations will stop during electrical storms.
  7. Models will fly only in predesignated areas, and will yield right-of-way to full-size aircraft.

Flying site operational rules

  1. Only pilots and assistants are allowed on the safety line.
  2. Mufflers are required on all models.
  3. Testing of engines is to be performed in specific designated areas.
  4. The AMA Safety Code will be displayed when the field is open for flying.
  5. Fliers operating models must display AMA membership cards for easy identification.
  6. Pilots shall make appropriate announcement when taking off, landing, or emergency situations.
  7. Compliance to these safety rules is the responsibility of each participant.
  8. It is strongly recommended that members do not fly alone.
  9. A First Aid Kit shall be kept in the equipment shed.
  10. No member or guest shall be under the influence of alcohol or illegal substances, or consume same at the site.
  11. Flight operations are allowed from designated areas only. Pilots may stand behind their plane upon take off, provided no other pilots are flying at that time. Pilots should move to the designated areas immediately following takeoff.
