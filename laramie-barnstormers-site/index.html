Welcome to the club

Three ways to enjoy the RC hobby in one location

The Laramie Barnstormers RC Club brings local enthusiasts together at a shared facility featuring a model airfield, an off-road RC track, and a dedicated crawler course.

Whether you are an experienced hobbyist or exploring radio control for the first time, contact the club before your first visit and we will help you get started.

Stark Field

Our facilities

One club location with space for flying, racing, and technical trail driving.

01

Model airfield

A canvas runway and open flying area for a variety of radio-controlled aircraft. New visitors should contact the club before flying.

02

RC track

An outdoor dirt track with turns,jumps, and room for off-road RC driving.

03

Crawler course

A technical course built from rock, brick, and natural terrain for scale crawlers and trail rigs.

Join the Barnstormers

Annual membership is $45

Membership supports operation and maintenance of the club facilities and connects you with other local RC enthusiasts.

  • Access to the club facilities
  • Club activities and community
  • Support for facility upkeep

Additional requirements may apply to airfield use. Contact the club for current membership and visitor information.
